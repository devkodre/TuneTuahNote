Welcome to TuneTuahNote

ToneTuahNote was started as a project for the premier hackathon at Purdue University: BoilerMake XII (in 2025).

What started as just an interactive piano evolved into a full fledged web application combining AI and music.

You can also record and stop recording and play back what you inputted.

Our main feature is a generative music tool where our website takes in your input and returns some generated music added to the end.
